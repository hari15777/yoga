About Us

Yoga is an ancient route to health and wellness. Indian Yoga School believes that everyone deserves the benefits of yoga as a practical tool of empowerment health and physical fitness.The first Yoga School providing authentic traditional Yoga Asanas,Breathing and Meditation Techniques.Come for regular Yoga classes,relax here with a cup of tea and read yoga related book, enhance your knowledge and practice more. Also offering Therapeutic Yoga and Some Certificate Course in Yoga......Our motto is" Yoga Is Accessible For All ".

Indian Yoga School at Yerevan founded in 2016 by Sri Hari who is a disciple of Swami Niranjanananda Saraswati.He has completed first 12 years phase of disipleship.Born in Varanasi,he started practicing Yoga as a seven year old,learning classical Aasanas and mantra chanting from his father,post a commerce degree,devoted full time to studying yoga from the world's first yoga university Bihar Yoga Bharati,Munger,India.Honoured with C.S.R. merit award first prize for "Value Of Yoga ".He has been teaching yoga in Delhi,Goa,Nepal and Armenia.He had also guided the world's first International Day Of Yoga 2015 in Yerevan,Armenia with association of the Indian Embassy, Armenia. .........here We offer daily practice of Vinyasa Flow(synchronised pre-set body movement with breath,to get in,be in and get out of Asanas),Hatha Yoga(specific yogic sequences practice) , Shivananda Yoga , Pranayama, Meditation, Yoga Nidra and many variations of Yoga branche. Offering Original Satyanand Yoga nidra(TM) (theory and practical classes ) for better sleep and a healthy mind.....

2.certificate course in hatha yoga :--

Hatha Yoga is a progressive practice that indicates specific yoga sequences .In order to achieve higher goal on the path of yoga first one need to keep body mind in a healthy and stable state .The practice of hatha yoga includes Aasana,Pranayama and Shatkarma,Mudra and Bandha.The practice starts from Aasana with few of shatkarma(suitable cleansing practices).Once the body achives a certain level of equilibrium the studet progress to Pranayama, Mudra and Bandhas.....Eligibility:-someone who does not have any serious injuries(spine,knee,ankle or bigger joints) no life taking physiological health conditions like serve cardiac conditions,or clinical depression. Intermediate aasana practice level-preferable hatha style.

3.Certificate Course in Yoga Nidra:-

Yoga Nidra is a consciousness between waking and sleep.it is one of the practice of "pratyahara"where awareness is internalised.when we are in a deep sleep,there remains a state of awareness where our consciousness is not connected to our sense and our receptivity is high.Eligibility:-one year of consistent asana&pranayama practice.reguler meditation practice and prior experience of yoga-nidra . 4.certificate course in Pranayama:-

Pranayama course for your practice/sadhna.Learn to explore your bigger dimension.Most people breathe incorrectly,using only a small part of their lung capacity.The breathing is then generally shallow. Rhythmic,deep and slow respiration helps achieve the calm and content state of mind. Pranayama is a practice in which a person utilizes breathing to influence the flow of prana in the NADIS (energy channels).Lifestyle has a profound impact on our prana.Irregularities in lifestyle ,dietary indiscretions and stress deplete the pranic flow and result is energy drain which can also lead to disease or metabolic malfunction.The techniques of pranayama reverse this process,energizing and balancing the different prana.Pranayama practice shall be performed after Aasana in a integrated Yoga Programme .........Eligibility;-at least 6 months of regular Aasana practice , no physical exertion during the pranayama course, and above 18 year of age.

taking up yoga as a career

As a yoga teacher,your physical capacity keeps on improving as and when you take more and more classes.This will improve your self awareness and deepens your knowledge.Yoga teaching involves interactions with people from different backgrounds and age groups.This enhances your social interaction and helps to understand the world in a better way.And be patient!! like any career, building a yoga career too will take some time of constant effort.be prepared to face all ups and downs,take everything positively and try your best to achieve your goal of becoming a successful and dedicated yoga teacher.

Meditation:-(yoga-nidra, antermouna, ajapa japa, review of the day)

1.Yoga Nidra :- It is an efficient way to obtain sleep.if you do not know how to practice yoga nidra,you can find original beginners,intermediate and advanced yoga nidra for you.also you can visit to our school for practice in a group class.

2.Antermouna:- through this pratyahara practice one become aware of inner thoughts and thought process.we become the observer, the witness of mind.witnessing is the the ability to take a step and observe thouhts,reactions and conditioning."your life is what you live in mind".this practice will help reduce the useless anxious thouhts and ring more constructive and positive thoughts.Observe and let go of those conditions that restrict you from becoming creative, innovativeand happy.

3.Ajapa japa;- ajapa japa is an ancient and simple meditation practice involving the awareness of breath,mantra and prana in the psychic pathway of the body.at this time when mind is full of fears and anxieties,ajapa japa is a soothing balm to calm the mind.

4.Review of the day:- this practice reflect over the day like a movie in rewind from the time you wake up in the morning to you go to sleep.This practice is a process of observing,adjusting and ultimately changing your behaviour. see the booklet/diary and review the day.

"Yogic Meditation is an ancient route to health and wellness.it can be practised anytime during the day,but meditation before sleep leads to a deeper and more relaxed sleep."Pratyahara" practice provides effective relief from depression and long standing anxiety. The result will be lower level of stress with more positive vibes for life."and It has been proven scientifically that daily practice can help ones natural balance and harmony bringing positive good health to all parts of your life .In the workplace yoga can be a great opportunity to reduce work stress and develop mind body fitness ,needed to complement today's fast paced lifestyle . During meditation your brain waves changes ,the result is lower level of stress,inhance physical and mental aptitude with more positive vibes in life.
